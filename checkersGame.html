Checkers

Instructions

Welcome to my game of checkers! Here are some guidelines and tips to help you understand the mechanics. Please read everything before you start to play.
  • At the start of a new game, it is always your turn.
  • Click on a piece to select it, then click on a landing spot.
  • There is no double jumping, and only a king can eat multiple pieces.
  • You cannot eat backwards unless your piece is a king.
  • Pieces that are kinged have an aqua-colored border around them.
  • King pieces can land anywhere when they jump an enemy piece aslong as a team member is not in their path.
  • If you need to select a different piece, un-select your current one by using the button at the bottom of the checker board.
  • While its the CPU's turn, please do not click on anything.
  • If you run into a glitch, unselect your piece or click on "Start a New Game".
  • Close this instruction window to begin.

    • Thanks for reading, I hope you enjoy!
